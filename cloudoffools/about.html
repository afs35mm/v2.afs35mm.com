Mission

Actors are at the center of the company. Cloud of Fools emerged out of the need to continue rigorous physical training under our master teacher, Niky Wolcz. We believe that a group of actors working and building a history together over a long period of time can instigate revelatory exchanges among our members and within our communities. We seek to deepen our understanding of the training exercises passed down to us and put them into practice in the form of new works - or known plays executed in new ways - in order to share Niky’s knowledge and expertise and vision for what theater can be, with our host presenters all over the world.

Members

Formerly called NU Classic Theatre and then Theatre Kuden, Cloud of Fools provides a stomping ground for graduates of Columbia University’s MFA Acting program to deepen training practices.

Cloud of Fools is Jon Froehlich, Susan Hyon, Laura Butler River, and Casey Robinson.
